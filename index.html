First Principles

The five truths of web-design.

  1. Convert attention into value.

    A website has one job. Every element either moves the sale forward, or it doesn’t belong.

  2. The body decides.

    They judge before they read. A feeling, not a thought. Lose them here and the words never get a turn.

  3. Every screen answers.

    Is this real? Is this for me? What now? Leave one open and they’re gone.

  4. Words convert; design directs.

    Words close the sale. Design just points the way. Pretty that says nothing sells nothing.

  5. Move the sale forward.

    Every element earns its place. Not taste. Not ego. It moves the sale, or it’s cut.
